emote\o/ app Privacy Policy (July 2013)

Our information collection and use practices are summarised below.

We receive information that:

- you provide when you register for and set up an account
- you post or content you share on emote
- you allow us to collect when you use certain emote features
- you allow social media services to share with us
- we get from seeing how you use emote
- we get from communicating with you
- we get from cookies and other technology

We use this information to:

- provide emote features and services to you
- manage and improve the emote app and services
- analyze how you use emote
- provide you with information
- diagnose technical problems and maintain security
- provide you with marketing that is relevant to you

We share this information with:

- other emote users where you allow information to be public
- social media services you tell us to share information with
- people who help us provide emote to you
- business partners, which may include advertising partners
- outside parties to comply with the law and legal obligations

We store information: primarily in secure locations with trustable and secure service providers
